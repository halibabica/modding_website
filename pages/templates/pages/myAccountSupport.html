Deleting account

If you would like to delete your account, contact us and we will remove all data that is associated with your account from our system. This includes mods, comments and any other submitted data.

Editing account

You can personalize your account by adding an avatar and a description. You can edit these by navigating to your profile. Please note that there is a 1000 character limit to your description, and your avatar will be scaled to 200x200.

You can also change your password on your profile page. Any other data cannot be changed unfortunately, and you will have to contact us to change any other data.
